Privacy Policy

Pasal Manager — Shop Management App

Last updated: April 2026

Summary: Pasal Manager stores your business data securely in the cloud (Supabase). We do not sell your data. We do not share it with anyone. Only you can see your data.

1. Who We Are

Pasal Manager is a business management application designed for shopkeepers in India and Nepal. The app helps you track sales, expenses, credit (udhaar), stock, staff, and generate financial reports.

2. What Data We Collect

We collect only the data you enter into the app:

  • Account: Your email address (used for login only)
  • Business data: Sales, expenses, udhaar records, stock items, supplier names, staff details, loan records — all entered by you
  • Settings: Shop name, country, language preference
  • Usage analytics: Anonymous visit logs (device type, browser, screen size) — no personal information

3. What We Do NOT Collect

  • We do not collect your real name, address, or government ID
  • We do not access your contacts, camera, microphone (except optionally for voice input with your permission)
  • We do not track your location
  • We do not show advertisements
  • We do not sell your data to any third party

4. How We Store Your Data

Your data is stored securely using Supabase (a trusted cloud database provider). All data is:

  • Encrypted in transit (HTTPS/TLS)
  • Protected by Row Level Security — only you can access your own data
  • Backed up automatically
  • Stored on servers in secure data centers

5. Who Can See Your Data

Only you can see your business data. We (the app developers) do not access your business records. Your data is private and only accessible with your login credentials.

6. Microphone Access (Voice Feature)

If you use the voice input feature, your microphone is accessed only while you are actively recording. Audio is processed locally on your device by the browser's built-in speech recognition — it is not sent to our servers.

7. Data Sharing

We do not share, sell, rent, or trade your personal or business data with any third party for any purpose.

The only exception is Supabase (our cloud storage provider), which stores your data on our behalf under their own privacy policy and security standards.

8. Data Deletion

You can delete all your data at any time:

  • Inside the app: Go to More → Settings → Clear All Data
  • To delete your account completely, contact us at the email below

9. Children's Privacy

Pasal Manager is designed for adults running businesses. We do not knowingly collect data from children under 13 years of age.

10. Changes to This Policy

If we make significant changes to this privacy policy, we will update the "Last updated" date at the top of this page. Continued use of the app after changes means you accept the updated policy.

11. Contact Us

If you have any questions about this privacy policy or want to request deletion of your data, contact us at:

Email: pasalmanager@gmail.com
